LAKE GARDEN

LEISURE PARK CYBERJAYA

A sprawling park with tree-lined walking trails, orchid and hibiscus displays, as well as a planetarium, Cyberjaya Lake Gardens offers a peaceful retreat where visitors can immerse themselves in nature, engage in recreational activities with family and friends. It's a haven for relaxation and rejuvenation in the heart of Cyberjaya.
