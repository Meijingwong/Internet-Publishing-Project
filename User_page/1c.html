A Day of Iconic Sights

  • Experience the modernity and beauty of Malaysia's administrative capital, Putrajaya, in this one-day trip package. Explore the architectural wonders, lush greenery, and serene lakes that define this planned city. From majestic government buildings to picturesque gardens and cultural landmarks, Putrajaya offers a unique blend of history, culture, and contemporary living.

  • Celcius Coffee Co.

    8 AM - 9 AM

    Start your day with a delicious breakfast at one of Putrajaya's charming cafes, such as Celsius Coffee Co. Enjoy some freshly brewed coffee and local specialties to fuel up for the day ahead.

    more

    Taman Botani Putrajaya

    9 AM - 12 PM

    Head to Taman Botani Putrajaya, also known as Putrajaya Botanical Garden, and spend the morning exploring the lush greenery, scenic flower fields, and serene lakeside views. Take a leisurely stroll through the garden paths, snap some photos, and immerse yourself in nature.

    more

    Albatross Coffee

    12 PM - 1 PM

    After your morning exploration, take a break for lunch at a nearby restaurant or café. You can sample some local Malaysian cuisine or opt for international dishes, depending on your preference.

    more

    Taman Saujana Hijau Putrajaya

    1 PM - 1.30 PM

    Next, head to Taman Saujana Hijau to experience the green tunnel park and enjoy a peaceful walk amidst the lush surroundings. Spend some time relaxing in the natural beauty of the park before continuing to Equestrian Park Putrajaya.

    more

    Equestrian Park Putrajaya

    1.30 PM - 3 PM

    Here, you can admire the impressive equestrian facilities and perhaps even watch a horse riding demonstration.

    more

    Tangga Putrajaya Steps

    3 PM - 3.30 PM

    Experience architectural splendor and scenic vistas at these iconic steps, a symbol of Malaysia's modernity and heritage. Marvel at the fusion of Islamic and contemporary design as you ascend, offering panoramic views of Putrajaya's stunning landscapes. Perfect for photo opportunities and a leisurely stroll, immerse yourself in the cultural tapestry of Malaysia at this must-visit destination.

    more

    iOi City Mall

    3.30 PM - 5 PM

    After your outdoor adventures, indulge in some retail therapy at one of Putrajaya's shopping centers, such as IOI City Mall. Browse the stores for souvenirs, clothing, or local handicrafts, and maybe pick up some gifts for friends and family back home.

    more

    DoubleTree by Hilton Putrajaya Lakesides

    5 PM - 7.30 PM

    Return to your hotel or accommodation for some relaxation and downtime. Take a refreshing shower, unwind with a book or a movie, or simply enjoy some quiet time before dinner.

    more

    Min Coffee Putrajaya

    7 PM onwards

    In the evening, head out for dinner at a local restaurant or café. You can choose from a variety of cuisines, ranging from Malaysian delicacies to international fare. After dinner, consider exploring Putrajaya's nightlife scene or attending a cultural performance or event if available.
