Mohd Amirudin @Amirul23

Very crowded in the morning, more relax in the evening. A place to wind down, to enjoy nature, or even endurance training, if you're into it, with the variation of terrain. Occasionally, there would be outdoor wedding photoshoot in the park. The best time to go (personally): just after the rain has stopped. A cool and misty environment, like Cameron Highlands if you will.

Ang CY @CYongew

This is a serene oasis, perfect for those seeking tranquility amidst nature. The lush greenery, well-maintained paths, and scenic lakes make it an ideal spot for leisurely walks or picnics. The thoughtful landscaping enhances the overall beauty of the park. Families, joggers, and nature enthusiasts will appreciate the peaceful ambiance.
